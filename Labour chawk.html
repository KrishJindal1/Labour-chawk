Frequently Asked Questions

We verify workers through a comprehensive process including ID verification, skill assessment, and background checks. All workers must provide valid government ID and undergo a verification process before joining the platform.

We support multiple payment methods including UPI, bank transfers, and digital wallets. All payments are processed securely through our escrow system to protect both workers and employers.

Our AI system matches workers based on multiple factors including skills, experience, location, and availability. It continuously learns from successful matches to improve recommendations.

We have a dedicated support team to handle disputes. Our escrow payment system ensures fair resolution, and we mediate between parties to reach a satisfactory solution.
